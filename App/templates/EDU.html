Our model is bullish on New Oriental Education (EDU) stock. This is not surprising, as the stock has seen a strong upward trend. It is up 32% YTD and 23% since it's pre-Coronavirus high.





We used a combination of 2 models to make our predictions. We used a Keras Long Short-Term model. We trained our data using stock prices from November 14, 2015 up until January 1, 2019. We used 120 days of stock data to forecast the rest of that time period. Our test period was the time period from January 1, 2019 to November 14, 2020. We again used 120 days of stock data (from January 1,2019 to October 1, 2019) to forecast the closing prices from October 1st to November 14th. These predicted prices are in blue on the graph. Then we trained a Keras TimeseriesGenerator on our predicted values. Everytime the TimeseriesGenerator predicted a new value, we would add that value to the set of inputs to the second model and remove the oldest data point. By the end, the inputs of the TimeseriesGenerator had been previously generated by itself.
